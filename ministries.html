Sunday School.

Practical, Bible-anchored teaching for every age group — from the littlest ones to our most seasoned saints.

Our Sunday School classes are where the Word gets unpacked in a way you can carry into Monday. Kids learn the Bible stories that will shape their lives. Teens wrestle with the real questions. Adults dig into Scripture together and leave equipped to live it out all week.

Youth · 12–18

Youth Ministry.

A place where teens are known, loved, challenged — and never talked down to.

Real conversations about faith, identity, dating, doubts, and the future. Real friendships forged over pizza and prayer. Real ministry opportunities where students lead and serve. Come for the fun; stay for the Jesus who actually meets you there.

Women of Guiding Light
Sisterhood

Women's Ministry.

Sisterhood rooted in Scripture — Bible studies, retreats, mentoring, and deep friendships forged in prayer.

Whether you're single, married, raising little ones, launching grown ones, or living the next chapter — you'll find women here who will cry with you, laugh with you, and walk with you. Monthly gatherings and annual retreats punctuate the year.

Connect →
Brotherhood

Men's Ministry.

Brotherhood, accountability, and growing into the men God called us to be — fathers, husbands, sons, leaders.

Early-morning Bible study, breakfast, fishing trips, service projects, and candid conversations about what it actually looks like to follow Christ in today's world. No fluff, no performance — just men sharpening men.

Outreach

Be the church —
in Camden County.

A church that turns inward starves. Here's how we turn outward — and how you can join us.

Ongoing

Food Pantry

Partnering with local organizations to get groceries to families in need, especially around holidays and tough seasons.

Back-to-School

Backpacks for Kids

Every August we send hundreds of students back to class equipped with the supplies they need — no child turned away.

Year-Round

Hospital & Shut-In Visits

Pastoral visits, prayer, communion, and a warm meal for those who can't make it to the sanctuary.

Seasonal

Community Day

A day each summer of free food, music, and fun — a gift to Camden County, no strings attached.

December

Christmas Blessings

Adopting local families and seniors for Christmas — gifts, groceries, and the good news of Emmanuel.

Partnerships

Global Missions

Supporting apostolic missionaries across the U.S. and abroad — sharing our blessing with the world.

A Warm Welcome

You were made for more than you know.

Whether you've walked with Christ for decades or you're just beginning to ask the big questions, there's a place at our table for you. Come as you are — and expect to be welcomed like family.

For more than sixty years, Guiding Light has been a spiritual home in northeastern North Carolina — grounded in the apostolic faith of Acts 2, lifted by passionate worship, and committed to loving our neighbors the way Christ first loved us.

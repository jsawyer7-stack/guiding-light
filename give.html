Why We Give

Sowing into eternity.

Every tithe and every offering is part of what keeps the lights on, the doors open, and the gospel going out — in Camden County and to the ends of the earth.

We believe generosity is a discipline of worship, not a fundraising campaign. So we teach Scripture honestly on giving and then trust the Lord to move hearts. If giving here doesn't align with where God is leading you, we bless you and celebrate your generosity wherever it lands.

"Every man according as he purposeth in his heart, so let him give; not grudgingly, or of necessity: for God loveth a cheerful giver."
2 Corinthians 9:7

Transparency

Where your gift goes.

We steward every dollar with care — because it's not ours. It's God's, entrusted through you.

40%

Ministry & Teaching

Pastoral staff, teaching resources, worship, and everything that happens on Sunday morning.

30%

Facilities

Utilities, maintenance, and keeping the sanctuary a warm, welcoming place for every gathering.

20%

Outreach & Missions

Local benevolence, food pantry, community events, and supporting apostolic missionaries.

10%

Next Generation

Youth retreats, Sunday school curriculum, scholarships, and the future God is shaping here.

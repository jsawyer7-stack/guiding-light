What to Expect

Your first Sunday.

Here's exactly what's going to happen — no surprises, no pressure, just a real welcome.

Step 01

Pull in, park easy.

Plenty of free parking right at the sanctuary. First-time visitor spots are near the main entrance — you can't miss it.

Step 02

Someone will say hello.

A greeter will meet you at the door, walk you through anything you need, and show you to a seat.

Step 03

Worship. Word. Welcome.

About 75 minutes of singing, Scripture, and a Bible-rich message. No pressure to participate in anything you're not ready for.

Step 04

Stick around.

After service we linger — talking, laughing, praying. Meet the pastor, grab coffee, ask anything you didn't get to ask online.
